Cats in Tights!

Let's play!

You'll be given a random number between 20-100.

Your goal is to match the number exactly by clicking on one of the kitties, with each one worth a different amount of points every round.

If you match the number exactly without exceeding it, you'll get a win! If you go over the given number, you'll get a loss.

Best of luck!
